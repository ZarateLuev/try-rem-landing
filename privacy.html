Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how REM ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service available at try-rem.com (the "Service"). By using REM, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who We Are

REM is operated by Oliver Luevano Zarate, based in Monterrey, Nuevo León, Mexico. You can contact us at support@try-rem.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We never store passwords in plain text.

2.2 Business Configuration

During onboarding, you provide information about your business including company name, industry, products, services, and operational details. This data is used solely to power your AI assistant and is never shared with third parties.

2.3 Communication Data

When REM is connected to platforms like WhatsApp, Messenger, or Instagram, we process messages exchanged between your business and your customers. This includes:

  • Message content (text, attachments)
  • Sender identifiers (phone numbers, user IDs)
  • Timestamps
  • Conversation history

This data is processed to enable AI responses, maintain conversation context, and provide analytics to you.

2.4 Technical Data

We automatically collect technical information such as IP addresses, browser type, device information, and usage patterns to ensure service reliability and security.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the REM service
  • Power your AI assistant with relevant business context
  • Enable communication across connected channels
  • Improve service quality and reliability
  • Respond to support inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We share information only with:

  • Meta Platforms (Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram): To enable messaging functionality through their official APIs.
  • AI Service Providers: We use Groq for natural language processing. Messages are processed transiently and not retained by these providers beyond the duration of the request.
  • Voice Synthesis Providers: We use ElevenLabs for voice generation when applicable.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Cloudflare and our hosting provider for delivering the service.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in databases protected by industry-standard encryption. Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 with salt. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@try-rem.com. Upon deletion request, we will remove your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@try-rem.com.

8. End-User Data (Your Customers)

If you use REM to communicate with your customers, you are responsible for ensuring you have appropriate consent and legal basis to process their data through our service. We process this data on your behalf as a data processor under your instructions.

9. Children's Privacy

REM is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than Mexico, including the United States, where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent changes. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Data Deletion Instructions

To delete your account and all associated data, send an email to support@try-rem.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" from the email associated with your account. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.
